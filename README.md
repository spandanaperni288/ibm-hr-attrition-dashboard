# IBM HR Attrition Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset to identify key factors contributing to employee turnover.  
The objective of this dashboard is to provide HR teams and management with data-driven insights to understand attrition patterns and high-risk employee segments.

---

## Dashboard KPIs
- Total Employees
- Total Attrition
- Attrition Rate (%)
- Average Salary
- Average Years at Company
- Highest Risk Segment

---

## Key Insights
- Employees with lower tenure show higher attrition rates.
- Certain job roles and departments experience higher turnover.
- Attrition trends vary across income levels and experience bands.
- Identifying high-risk segments supports proactive retention planning.

---

## Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers for interactivity
- KPI Cards
- Data Cleaning and Transformation

---

## Business Value
This dashboard enables:
- Monitoring of employee turnover trends
- Identification of high-risk segments
- Data-driven retention strategy development
- Improved workforce planning decisions
